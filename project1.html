Our Summer Memories

Medium: Clip Studio Paint, Scratch, Medibang Paint


This project is a collection featuring the same characters and story. There is an animation
made on Clip Studio Paint, as well as a Scratch game.

Link to animation

Link to game

The animation was made for my animation class. We were free to use whichever medium we wanted to,
so I decided to make it fully digital. I did consider incorporating physical aspects, and even started
making clay figures. The clay figures are of the main characters. This was scratched and I just decided
to make it only digital.

The animation went through a stage of rough drafting. I then lined each frame and decided to lightly
color it. I didn't wanna make it too complicated for myself, as it is my first animation. I went for a more
minimalistic look, and it matches the simple meaning the series has.

The series follows two young characters, who like each other. However, the focus is more about the memories
they made one summer. It is meant to represent the longing of a more simpler time. Looking back to the past
with endearment, wishing those times never ended. It is based on my own feelings towards the past.

The game follows the same premise. However, you start off playing as the girl. The guy is late, but she must
still go to their meeting spot. There are a few minigames on the way there. Such as taking out the trash, avoiding
crabs and collecting shells. Once you get to that meeting spot, you play as the guy. You must accompany him to
the spot.
