My Feelings Towards You

Medium: Renpy, Clip Studio Paint

This project is a small story, in the format of a visual novel. It is made in Renpy.

My first decision was to choose the software. I decided on Ren’py, because it is quite easy to use and free.
I hesitated between choosing Twine, but Ren’py allows for more media to be inserted. I did plan on drawing
and adding my own art. I decided to make the art more sketch-like, to convey a specific aesthetic.

I wanted to create a dating sim that is similar to a classic romance animation. However, I didn’t know who’s
point of view I wanted, so I made the options to do both. The story follows two comical characters that follow
specific tropes, and are meant to parody them. The guy is a very blunt, unemotional guy who isn’t very expressive.
He actually doesn’t care much for in general, and proceeds with his life while trying to minimize any effort. The girl
is rather flustered and shows many emotions on her face, while not necessarily wanting to. Many of her words contradict
her actual feelings.

I wanted to make a simple game with a cute story. I enjoy romance stories with similar dynamics, so it was nice to
try to create my own. I wanted people to feel the same way, to be entertained and finding it cute as well.
