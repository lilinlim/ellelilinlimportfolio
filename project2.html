A Serving of Soup

Medium: Womp3D

This project is a 3D model made in Womp3D.

Link to model

I have never used the software prior to this project. I have always wanted to start more 3D modeling,
and found out about this software that made it more approachable. I made the work to familiarize myself
with 3D creating softwares. I also have a harder time with creativity. I tend to make works that are
concrete. I don’t do much abstract art. I used this as an opportunity to to get out of my comfort zone
and make something different.try something different.


When creating each object, I tried to incorporate different shapes. I used some concrete ones, such as
cherries and clouds, while some aren’t actually objects. I also made some more random, flexible objects
and wanted to mix and play around with different colors and materials.
