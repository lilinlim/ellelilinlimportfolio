Alien

Medium: P5


This project is a generative art piece. The alien is not random, however the background is.
This piece is supposed to represent how we sometimes feel out of place, like an alien.
This is my first project made in p5, and I followed a tutorial to familiarize myself with the
random functions and how strong p5 can really be.
